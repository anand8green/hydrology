Benefits

Simple billing, competitive prices and excellent customer service.

Simple dashboards

We offer a tailored dashboard on both our mobile and web apps, this highlights your current usage for this period compared to the last period and shows all notifcations on your account in one place.

24/7 account support

We are available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week via live chat and via e-mail. We keep historic conversations and have a dedicated help centre to make sure you're always in the know with your energy.

Forecasting & insights

In our app we will analyse your usage and payment history to provide you with a predication on your bills and an overview on how your usage trends change throughout the months.

Intelligent payments

Direct debit payments which analyse your usage and suggests a more accurate payment over time and Apple/Google Pay integration for topping up your account with credit.
